My top 3 movies of all-time.

Finding Nemo

I love this movie because the determination Marlin has to find his son resonates with me because it shows he's willing to do anything to save his son.

Lion King

Lion King is one of the classics of Disney and it takes themes from Hamlet. I really enjoyed this movie and it used to be my number 1 before Finding Nemo came along.

Beauty and The Beast

The theme of love is what really caputered my attention here and it showed me that anyone can change because as we see the Beast is a bad person until Belle helps him change his ways.
